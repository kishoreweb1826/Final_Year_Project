Frequently Asked Questions

To register as a farmer, visit our Farmer Registration page. You'll need to provide your farm details and upload your government-issued organic certification. Our team will verify your credentials within 24-48 hours.

We deliver fresh produce within 24 hours of order placement. Delivery slots are available between 8 AM - 8 PM. You can select your preferred time slot during checkout.

All our farmers hold valid certifications from recognized bodies like NPOP, PGS-India, or equivalent international standards. We verify all certificates before onboarding farmers.

We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible locations.

Our AI tools use machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of farm datasets. Visit our AI Tools page to access crop recommendations, resource management, and more.
